Our Culture Statement

HIPPOS: Humility, Impact, People, Partnership, Openness, Simplicity

  • Humility: We lead with humility in everything we do. We are new kids on the block and do not take that lightly. Every misstep is a lesson, every challenge a chance to grow.
  • Impact: Impact comes before profit, always. The work we do matters because of the difference it makes, not the revenue it generates. Our users pay not just for tools, but for meaningful, measurable change in their workflows, communities, and lives.
  • People: We value people first. In an age dominated by AI, our edge isn’t just code, but it’s authentic connection. We are relentlessly people-first, designing systems and experiences that honor human creativity, curiosity, and collaboration.
  • Partnership: Great ideas never happen in isolation. They emerge at the intersection of human insight and AI intelligence. We facilitate everything we do in a spirit of collaboration and mutual growth.
  • Openness: Openness is both our philosophy and our practice. We are open-source, platform-agnostic, and believe in open communication and feedback between people of all backgrounds, ages, and titles.
  • Simplicity: Radical accessibility is non-negotiable. Whether it’s someone vibe-coding for the first time or an AI researcher at Caltech, MemMachine should feel intuitive, delightful, and instantly empowering. Simplicity means everyone can meaningfully engage without friction.
